What is swatching?

Swatching is a term used to describe the process of "testing" products. For example if you put eye shadow on the back of your hand to see how it looks on your skin, you are swatching the eye shadow. Nail swatching means applying the polish to the nail without adding anything else, for example nail art on top of the nail polish. Swatching and swatch pictures are good when you need to know how a certain product looks with your skin tone or how the formula behaves. Often the color may look very different in the bottle versus on your nails!

See below my recent swatches:

Heavy Metal Mixtape by Holo Taco

Holo Taco is a brand by famous Youtuber Simply Nailogical. 'Heavy Metal Mixtape' is from their recent Remix Collection. The polish is described as a gunmetal color with metallic foil formula. The polish applied well and I only needed two coats. The color is dark grey, almost black and in some lighting it looks almost purple! I really love this one, it was my favourite of the collection!

Glitterally Purple by Holo Taco

'Gliterally Purple' is from Holo Taco's Remix Collection. This polish is a light purple glitter formula. Holographic glitter formulas usually have a clear base, like this polish, so that the holo glitter is able to shine through better. Glitter formulas can also be very difficult to remove, so using a peel off base coat is recommended! This polish is jam-packed with different color and size glitters, and it sparkles so beautifully in every lighting!

Rainbow Flood by Holo Taco

'Rainbow Flood' is from Holo Taco's Remix Collection. This polish is a darker purple with linear holographic formula. Linear holographic means the tiny holo glitters line up on the nail, creating a shine that is more uniform than with scattered holographic glitter. This polish is described as a aubergine aKa eggplant purple.
