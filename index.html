Gini who?

I've been doing nail art for a couple of years now, and my nail art obsession deepened during the pandemic.

I don't use acrylics or other types of fake nails, and rarely use gel nail polishes. Not because I think there's anything wrong with them, I just like changing my nails so often that I prefer regular nail polish! So everything you see here will be done using my natural nails and regular air-dry nail polishes!

I identify as nail polish addict (NPA) and holosexual - which if you don't know is a term created by Simply Nailogical meaning someone who reaaally likes holographic nail polish! Drug store polish could never xD I hope you like it here, and I hope to see you over at my other socials!
