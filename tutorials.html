Tutorials

Sometimes reading about how to create a nail art look is just not informative enough. Luckily tutorial videos are relatively easy to make. Filming them always isn't, but that's another story entirely!

I create tutorial videos for Instagram, Youtube and TikTok. Below you can see a couple of examples.

Further down you'll find links to more tutorials on other platforms!

Holo Dagger Nails

This is recreation look of a classic Simply Nailogical design. I did it for a Simply Birthday collab back in 2020.

Here you'll need two different nail polishes, a sparkly nail topper and some striping tape or you could just use a regular tape too.

Teal Leopard Look

This is a fun design because you can achieve it by using a dotting tool, or just a toothpick!

Very easy to do, and you can make it interesting by switching up the colors. You could make each nail a different base color, or then create a rainbow of dots on each nail, the possibilities are endless!

St. Patrick's Day Nails

Here is a stamping design I did for St. Paddy's Day back in 2021.

For this look you will need gold and green polishes, and stamping equipment. I had a stamping plate with shamrocks, but you could also freehand the design if you don't have the stamping gear!
